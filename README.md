# SkyNet - Diffusion Models

This work was part of a term project for the UMC203 Introduction to AI and ML course at IISc.

## Introduction

We plan to explore the principles behind diffusion models, a class of generative models that are emerging as a transformative tool in modern machine learning, allowing a broad spectrum of interdisciplinary applications that include realistic or artistic text to image synthesis, text to video generation, predictive modeling of molecular structures such as proteins [Jumper et al.2021](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03819-2) and pharmaceutical drugs.

We also plan to conduct various experiments to understand and verify the results stated in various papers on diffusion models.
